About

Grammy-nominated musician Dan Policar delivers bold, immersive scores for video games, film, and big brand advertising. His work is built on emotional storytelling, blending non-traditional techniques with tried-and-true methods.

He has composed music for major titles including Empire of Sin (Romero Games), The Crimson Diamond (Julia Minamata), and the upcoming Ifeyo's Adventure (Transolar Games). Over his career, Dan has had the opportunity to collaborate with legendary developers including Ken and Roberta Williams, and Brenda and John Romero.

A Unique Global Sound: In addition to his composing career, Dan has toured internationally for over a decade as a keyboardist for dancehall superstar Sean Paul. This experience infuses his scores with rhythmic vitality, Jamaican culture, and a unique global flavor, making his sound instantly memorable.

Dan also composes live on his channel twitch.tv/dantheband, fostering creative input and promoting the projects in progress.
